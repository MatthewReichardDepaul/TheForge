For this week, the assignment was to create a simple, rough design of one of our project ideas and display (or render) the design in different formats including 3D, engineering drawing, 2D sketch, and anything else we can come up with. Below you can see several stages of rendering for my simplified design of my 'plant care table'.


After my initial drawing, I created a basic 2D sketch outline in Fusion, then extruded it into 3D and created the basic model. Within fusion, I created an engineering drawing of the model and a simple 3D image render as well. Finally, I imported the model into Blender and used some other 3D assets to create a realistic speculative render of the project.

Finally, I realized part of the assignment was to create a video of me making one of these renders, so I needed to make a new render. I filmed a video inside my dorm and used Blender's camera tracking to place my 3D model into the video. You can see that and the timelapse of me creating the render below.
