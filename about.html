Project Ideas

  • Automated Plant Pot
    • Receptical for plant
    • Uses camera tied to image recognition library to determine plant type
    • Plugs plant type into MLM to output information such as watering frequency and light needs
    • Waters on a timer (x amount of times per week) and collects light data with a light meter and determines if lighting conditions are ideal after a day
    • If the light source is a window, the pot will rotate automatically to even out plant growth
  • All-Day Light-Based MIDI Melody
    • Several light sensors that record data at set intervals over a set amount of time
    • Performs processes (based on light amount and color) to assign a note to each interval withing a selected key
    • Generates a midi sequence that is either stored to an SD card or is played directly through a midi output port
  • Planter Table
    • A cube-shaped table with a plexiglass top which houses a plant inside
    • Utilizing similar technology to the 'Automated Plant Pot' idea
    • Plugs plant type into MLM to output information such as watering frequency and light needs
    • Waters on a timer (x amount of times per week) and sets glow lights on and off based on plant's needs
  • Mood Speaker
    • An all-in-one speaker box that plays music to match the mood at the appropriate volume
    • Uses light meter to determine the intensity and colors of the light in the room to determine 'mood'
    • Connects to music streaming service and plays music based on the 'mood'
    • Uses a microphone to determing the volume of the background noise in the room and sets speaker volume to just over that level
    • Mood lights on the speaker also change and adapt to the light in the room
